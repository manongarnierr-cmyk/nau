How it works

Two ways
to be part of Nau

Whether you own a boat or dream of sailing one, there's a place for you in our community.

⚓
Nau Swap
For boat owners

You have a boat in Barcelona. I have one in Dubrovnik. Let's trade for the summer — no money, just reciprocity and trust between sailors.

  • Unlimited boat exchanges
  • Points system — no need to swap simultaneously
  • Integrated temporary insurance
  • Skipper verification on every member
  • Flash rental if no match available
⛵
Nau Crew
For crew & adventurers

A boat owner is heading to the Costa Brava and opens a spot on board. You share the real costs — fuel, port, food. No rental, no bullshit.

  • Access to all crew postings in Med
  • Share real costs only — not a rental
  • Meet verified, passionate sailors
  • Day trips to multi-week crossings
  • Barcelona · Balearics · Costa Brava
